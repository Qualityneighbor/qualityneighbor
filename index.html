🔥 Beat the Heat, Ranch-Style: Smart Moves for Hot Days

June in Central Texas brings crunch time, lake life, and ranch chores galore. Whether you're working the pond, chasing livestock, or enjoying a BBQ, staying cool can make a world of difference.

  1. Time Your Activities Wisely: Work or exercise during the cooler parts of the day. Avoid working at midday. And if sun-showers hit, it's a sign to rest and chill.
  2. Dress for the Heat: Lightweight, light-colored, and moisture-wicking clothing. Protect kids from long-sleeve sun exposure. Wide-brimmed hats keep you shady.
  3. Stay Hydrated: Hydrate before, during, and after physical activity. Ranchers know that “water is wealth.” Try adding a splash of citrus or electrolytes to replenish lost minerals.[...]
  4. Take Regular Breaks: Chores should never mean exhaustion. Set a timer so you rest pockets. This is especially important for pets, too!
  5. Know the Signs of Heat-Related Wellness: Watch for symptoms: excessive sweating, fatigue, and rapid heart rate. If you or a child feel faint, seek medical attention if necessary.

Bonus tip: Drink lots of water while keeping yourself and your animals cool!
